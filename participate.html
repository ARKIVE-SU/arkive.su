How to Participate

No financial commitment. No immediate obligations. Just a shared belief that knowledge should outlast us all.

Three Ways to Contribute

Curatorial Node

For: Libraries, archives, museums, digital repositories

What you provide

Curatorial expertise and access to collections. You select what matters most.

What you receive

Your collections preserved on the most durable media ever created. Visibility in an international network of preservation institutions.

Commitment

None financial. Only interest and willingness to engage in dialogue.

Technology Partner

For: Developers of ultra-durable storage media

What you provide

Storage technology and technical expertise. Capacity allocation for encoding.

What you receive

Direct access to the world's largest archival institutions. Real-world use cases for your technology.

Commitment

Technical collaboration. No financial obligations.

Infrastructure Partner

For: Space companies, bunker operators, vault facilities

What you provide

Physical space and logistics for long-term placement of preserved media.

What you receive

Participation in a landmark project. PR visibility. A network of leading institutions and technology developers.

Commitment

Space/payload allocation. No financial obligations.

Expression of Interest

A simple "we are open to the idea" is exactly what we need at this stage. Every expression of interest strengthens the case for the entire network.
